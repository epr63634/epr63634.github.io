The Roberge Story.

Hey my name is Evan Roberge I am a junior here at the University of Georgia. Going to the University of Georgia has always been a dream of mine and to be here when Georgia football is at an all time high is awesome. I am a big fan of sports. I love all of the Atlanta teams, the falcons, hawks, braves, and now the new soccer team Atlanta United. I am also a fan of the UConn men’s basketball team, my father went there so I grew up watching them play. My favorite team out of all of those however is the Georgia bulldogs. I follow them very closely and I get very defensive when people talk poorly of them. We recently got commitments from the #1 overall player in the 2018 class and both the #1 and #3 ranked running backs, so our future is looking bright. Right now I am a finance major in the terry college of business and I’m excited to finally start learning about what I want to learn about. I’m hopeful that shortly after I graduate I’ll have a job in a major city like Chicago or New York. If that doesn’t work out then I’d like to have a job that requires traveling, however; I would only want to travel for work for a few years. Another thing that I am very interested in is fishing. Every time my family takes a vacation me and my dad always go deep sea fishing or we go fishing in the bay. The most recent time we went fishing we were in Warwick Rhode Island and my dad, my uncle and my cousin all went fishing for stripped bass. At one point we were catching anywhere from 4-8 fish at a time.
